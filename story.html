About Us

JPrince Catering Service

A few years down the track, we have grown organically and expanded to offering different ethnic cuisines for weddings,birthdays, corporate and private events.

JPrince is a high end caterer serving the Nigerian state. We deliver premium food and event experiences to our clientele by focusing on Design, Presentation, Taste, and Variety.

Our attention to details, thoughtful process is to provide creative solutions for our clients which differentiate us from others in the industry.
